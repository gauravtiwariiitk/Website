Gaurav Tiwari

Associate Professor

Hi! I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. I lead the Engineering Rock Mechanics Lab and my research primarily focuses on experimental and numerical analyses of rock structures.

Biography

I received my bachelor's degree from MBM Govt Eng. College Jodhpur, my M.Tech from IIT Roorkee in 2013 (Advisor: Narendra Kumar Samadhiya) and PhD degree from IISc Bengaluru (Advisor: Madhavi Latha G). Prior to joining IIT Kanpur, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at IISc Bengaluru.

  • Associate Professor, Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Kanpur (2024 – Present)
  • Assistant Professor, Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Kanpur (2018 – 2024)
  • Post-Doctoral Research Associate, IISc Bangalore (2018)
