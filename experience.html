Current Research Interests and Ongoing Work

Current Research Interestsüîç

Our group works on the themes of information centric uncertainty modelling of rock properties based on stochastic and non-stochastic modelling of rock properties, and hybrid reliability methodologies to deal with these uncertainties. Other areas are application of Bayesian Multi-Model Inference for sensitivity/reliability analyses in rock engineering, THMC modelling of rocks for energy storage purposes and grain scale modelling of rocks. In the experimental rock mechanics, we are currently looking for dynamic response of rocks and couples it with DIC, fragmentation analyses to understand the associated mechanisms.
